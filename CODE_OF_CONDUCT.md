# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give technical feedback with clear, actionable context.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Trolling or personal attacks.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report incidents through private maintainer channels or repository contact methods.
